# Pinakes — Product context

register: product

## Product purpose
Native Android client for a Pinakes library instance. A member browses the
catalog, checks availability, borrows/reserves books, reads ebooks and listens to
audiobooks, and manages their loans, wishlist and profile. The app SERVES the
task: find a book, see if it's free, borrow it. The UI must disappear into that
task.

## Users
Library members on their own phone, mostly in daylight or normal indoor light,
in a calm "browsing / reading" mood. Not power users; not a dark-room tool.

## Brand & tone
Pinakes. One signature colour: **magenta `#D70161`** (the palm logo). Tone:
quiet, precise, trustworthy, a little warm. Think a good reading app, not a
flashy consumer social app.

## Strategic principles
- **Minimal, not decorated.** Content (book covers, titles, availability) is the
  hero. Chrome recedes.
- **One accent.** Magenta is the only colour with meaning: primary actions,
  current selection, key status. Everything else is a clean neutral. No second
  brand hue, no indigo/violet, no rainbow chips.
- **Readable first.** Real contrast on every label and field. Never colour-on-
  near-colour.
- **Light by default.** A member checking a book in daylight wants a calm white
  surface. Dark is available, not assumed.

## Anti-references (what this must NOT look like)
- Gradients of any kind (hero banners, headers, buttons). Banned.
- Purple/indigo selection states. The "random colours" look. Banned.
- Warm brown/dun surfaces. Neutrals stay cool and clean.
- Decorated circular logo badges, ringed avatars, heavy cards everywhere.
- "AI slop": a washed-out gradient panel with light text on a light field.
